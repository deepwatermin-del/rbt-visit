1. Information We Collect

Rome Baptist Temple collects personal information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit our Plan a Visit form at https://deepwatermin-del.github.io/rbt-visit/
  • Fill out a Facebook Lead Ad form for Rome Baptist Temple
  • Contact us directly by phone, email, or in person

Information collected may include: full name, phone number, email address, and general location.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Follow up regarding your interest in visiting or attending Rome Baptist Temple
  • Send you information about service times, events, and church programs
  • Provide pastoral care and personal follow-up communications

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Text Message (SMS) Communications

By providing your phone number through our Plan a Visit form or Facebook Lead Ad, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Rome Baptist Temple.

  • Types of messages: Welcome messages, event reminders, service announcements, and pastoral follow-up
  • Message frequency: Varies; typically 2–4 messages per month
  • To opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive one confirmation message and no further texts.
  • For help: Reply HELP or call (706) 235-1515
  • Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Rome Baptist Temple does not share your phone number or any opt-in data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
