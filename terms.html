1. Program Description

Rome Baptist Temple operates an SMS messaging program to communicate with individuals who have expressed interest in our church. Messages may include welcome messages, service and event information, and pastoral follow-up communications.

2. How to Opt In

You opt in to receive SMS messages from Rome Baptist Temple by voluntarily submitting your phone number through one of the following:

  • The Plan a Visit form at https://deepwatermin-del.github.io/rbt-visit/
  • A Rome Baptist Temple Facebook Lead Ad form
  • Directly contacting Rome Baptist Temple and providing your phone number

By submitting your phone number through any of the above methods, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Rome Baptist Temple at the number provided.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 4 messages per month. Rome Baptist Temple will not send unsolicited bulk messages.

4. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. These charges are billed by and payable to your mobile service provider. Rome Baptist Temple does not charge for SMS messages.

5. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages from Rome Baptist Temple at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.

You may also opt out by calling (706) 232-8969 and requesting to be removed from our SMS list.
