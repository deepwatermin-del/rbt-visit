Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 3, 2026

Rome Baptist Temple ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information you provide when you contact us, submit forms, or opt in to receive communications from our church.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with Rome Baptist Temple through our website forms, Facebook Lead Ads, or in-person contact:

  • First and last name
  • Phone number (mobile)
  • Email address
  • Address (if provided)
  • How you heard about our church

SMS / Text Message Communications

By opting in to receive text messages from Rome Baptist Temple, you consent to receive automated SMS notifications to the mobile number you provided.

Rome Baptist Temple sends SMS text messages to individuals who have voluntarily opted in through one of the following methods:

  • Submitting the "Plan a Visit" form at our website
  • Submitting a Facebook Lead Ad form
  • Verbally providing consent to a church staff member or volunteer

Messages sent may include:

  • Welcome and visitor follow-up messages
  • Service times and event reminders
  • Pastoral care and outreach communications
  • General church announcements

Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on your interaction with our church. You may receive up to a few messages per week during active outreach periods.

Message and data rates may apply. Standard messaging rates from your mobile carrier may apply to messages you send and receive.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any text message at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

To get help: Reply HELP to any text message or contact us at the information below.

We do not sell, trade, or share your phone number or personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely to communicate with you about Rome Baptist Temple's ministry, services, and events.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to:

  • Follow up with prospective visitors and guests
  • Send church event and service notifications
  • Provide pastoral care and ministry support
  • Communicate about church programs and outreach

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for commercial purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or as required by applicable law. You may request removal of your information at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Request that your personal information be deleted from our records
  • Ask what information we have on file for you
